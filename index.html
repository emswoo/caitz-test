LOVE IS IN THE HAIR

BIO

Caitlin Keyser is passionate hair stylist, makeup artist with an eye for fashion and unique looks and is creative behind Jol Salon in San Diego.

Caitlin's talents are extensive — stemming from diverse history of modeling, editorial styling and photography.
Caitlin has had the opportunity to study and train at Paul Mitchell US.
This unique experience assists Caitlin in employing the very latest techniques to enhance his ork and create up to date, tailored results for all his clients.

Her speciality are precision cutting techniques combined with razor texturing, personalizing results for a modern fluid look.

Caitlin's passion is driven by a simple philography to bring out the inner beauty in everyone she encounters.
